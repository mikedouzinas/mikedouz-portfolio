# Portal window images

These are the "vibe" photos that show **behind the frosted glass** of the portal
window (the entrance to THE HARLEQUIN). The window picks one at **random** per load,
softens it behind frost, and shows a caption.

## How to add photos (easiest way)

1. In Finder, open this folder: `mikedouz-portfolio/public/portal/`
2. **Drag your images in.** That's it — drop them right here.
3. **Name them descriptively, lowercase, with dashes** — the filename becomes the caption:
   - `greece.jpg` → "Greece"
   - `tame-impala.jpg` → "Tame Impala"
   - `me-and-gabe.jpg` → "Me and Gabe"
   - `night-out-with-friends.jpg` → "Night out with friends"
   (I auto-prettify the filename into the on-screen caption, so no JSON to edit.)

## What kind of photos

They sit **behind frost** (softened, not sharp), so pick **evocative, color-rich vibe
shots** — mood over detail. Avoid busy or text-heavy images (lost behind the frost).

Your starter set: Spider-Man · night out with friends · you + the number 12 ·
Tame Impala concert · you alone at a bench · you + Gabe · La Mena Mall · Greece.

## Format

- **Landscape** orientation (fits the window best).
- ~**1200–1600px** on the long edge.
- Optimized **JPG or WebP** (~150–300 KB each).
- Mixed sizes are fine — the window crops with `object-fit: cover`.
- ~8–15 images to start; add more anytime.

Once they're in, tell me and I'll wire the window to read this folder (random pick +
caption + cover-fit behind the frost).
